ABOUT US

Pioneer Trust is a pioneer in the field of education and was founded in 1997 by Sri. G. Devarajan and Smt. D. Suguna. The Founder Trustee Smt. D. Suguna is an eminent Educationist and Philanthropist. Education for knowledge and personality building has been her motive and she has been well groomed, through the years, in this field by her experienced elders like Sri.G.R.Govindarajulu and Sri.G.K.Devarajulu. Smt. Aparna Rajkumar is the current Managing Trustee of Pioneer Trust and Secretary of Pioneer College of Arts and Science. The college serves the higher education needs of predominantly rural areas of Coimbatore and Nilgiris districts. Ooty is just 60 Kms away from the campus. Coimbatore, an industrial city, is known for its higher educational institutions in Arts, Science, Medicine, Engineering and Technology and Management. Pioneer College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore is run by Pioneer Trust in a green campus located just outside of Coimbatore City in Tamil Nadu. Courses in Computer Science, Commerce and Business Management streams are offered by the college. Pioneer CAS, recognised by UGC and accredited by NAAC, offers Undergraduate programmes, Postgraduate programmes and Research programmes under the aegis of Bharathiar University, Coimbatore
